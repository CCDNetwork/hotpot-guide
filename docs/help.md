## Escalation Path

If you encounter a problem that is not answered in this guide, or if you are completely stuck, please follow these steps to get help:

1. **Take a screenshot** of the problem or error message you are seeing.
2. **Note your Case Number** (if the problem is related to a specific referral or booking).
3. **Contact your organisation's administrator** and provide them with the screenshot, your Case Number, and a brief description of what you were trying to do.

!!! note "Contact Information"
    If your organisation has a dedicated support email or helpdesk link, you can reach out to them directly using that channel.
